Hello Friends!

My name is Kelly Kobarda and I'm a Graphic Designer. I have always loved being creative and using my imagination to solve problems. I have always enjoyed generating art and working with different types of media. After years of experimenting, I decided graphic design is where I wanted to focus my creative energy.

I have a Certificate of Art and Design Fundamentals from Sheridan College, as well as an Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design from Fanshawe College. As of right now, I'm working at Postmedia as a Senior Graphic designer for top revenue-generating newspapers.

Please take the time to look at what I have accomplished on my portfolio page. I have a variety of things to show you.
